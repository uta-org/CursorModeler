[English] ComixCursors Black & Red. More colors soon... !!!!
Edited and optimized for Windows users [...well, actually I use Linux xP]. Tested on Win7, Win-Vista and XP

How to install... ? 
1. Extraxt the folder anywhere
2. Find  the file Install_[.inf] into the folder extracted
3. Right - Click, and click in the 'Install' option
*4. Go to Control Panel > Hardware and Sound > Mouse > Tab Pointers and select in Schemes ComixCursors Black and Red [If you don't use Win7, go to Control Panel > Mouse]
5. Apply.... & Ready!! xP
